Data Science and Analytics Projects

Exploring H-1B Visa Wage Trends

Applied advanced regression models to study wage factors across industries and regions for H-1B visa holders. Conducted data cleaning, feature engineering, and comparative analysis across economic sectors to identify wage discrepancies and regional effects.

Cricket Match Outcome Prediction

Built machine learning models using player fatigue metrics, historical match data, and game dynamics to predict cricket match outcomes with enhanced accuracy. Integrated real-time data ingestion to improve model responsiveness.

Health Data Analysis for Smoking Cessation Programs

Utilized logistic regression and survival analysis models to predict smoking cessation outcomes. Worked with longitudinal health survey datasets, emphasizing predictive health analytics and early intervention strategy evaluation.

COVID-19 Spread Modeling Using Public Mobility Data

Analyzed the relationship between public mobility patterns and COVID-19 spread across U.S. regions. Applied time series analysis and statistical modeling to identify key mobility factors influencing case growth rates.

Labor Economics Applications

Intergenerational Mobility and Local Labor Markets

Investigated the relationship between local economic conditions and intergenerational economic mobility. Analyzed linked census and IRS data to reveal regional disparities and proposed policy interventions.

Minimum Wage Policy Impact Analysis

Evaluated the causal effects of minimum wage increases on youth employment and income distribution using quasi-experimental methods. Synthesized results across multiple U.S. states to inform labor policy debates.

Education and Labor Market Outcomes

Explored how variations in educational attainment levels impact wage trajectories and career progression across industries. Utilized longitudinal labor market data and advanced econometric techniques.
